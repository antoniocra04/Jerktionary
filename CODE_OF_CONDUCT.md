# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Jerktionary to be a practical, respectful project for users and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, technical, and respectful.
- Focus criticism on code, design, and behavior.
- Assume mistakes can be fixed through clear discussion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.
